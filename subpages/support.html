PlayerOne is a cloud-based gaming library. One of its most popular features is the ability for users to use any computer to play games they buy/download to their PlayerOne accounts. This also allows users to store a large collection of games without using too much computer memory.

To use PlayerOne, you have to download and install the PlayerOne "engine" or app on your computer. Once you do that, you can access the full library of games, software, and forums. Easily track and monitor your active friends through phones.
